What’s Covered

Fire

Total or partial damage to the commercial vehicle caused by incidents of fire or explosion.

Natural and Man-made Calamities

Damage caused to the vehicle due to earthquakes, landslides, cyclones, floods, or even riots, vandalism, etc.

Theft

Loss or damage because of the vehicle being stolen.

Accidents

Damage to the commercial vehicle caused due to an accident.

Personal Accident

Injury or death of the driver in case of an accident of the commercial vehicle.

Third-party Loses

Damages caused to a third party by the commercial vehicle.

What’s Not Covered

Drunk Driving

Accidents caused due to the owner-driver driving under the influence of alcohol.

Driving without a valid Driving Licence

Accidents caused due to the owner-driver driving without a valid license.

Not Covered by Add-ons

Risks that have not been covered by specific add-ons.

Contributory Negligence

Damage due to the carelessness of the driver.

Consequential loss

Consequential damage that is not a direct result of accident/ natural calamity, etc. damage causes subsequent damage/loss, only the original damage will be covered.
